FAQ

You've got questions? We've got answers.

  • How can I approach a neighbor?

    Hi my name is ______ I am here to help with whatever assistance you need during the hurricane. I am working with this program to assist my neighbors during the hurricane.



  • What personal information do you need to keep them weather active?

    Phone number, email, text or all three

  • What can I do to to help after the hurricane?

    Ask your neighbors if they need water shelter, etc.
